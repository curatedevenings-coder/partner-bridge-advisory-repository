What this is

This is a curated advisory service for situations where experience matters more than volume. If you need a clear view on a licensing decision, a valuation question, a commercial tradeoff, or a partnership path, I help you reach the right expert quickly.

I do the matchmaking and the scoping myself. That means the call is prepared around your actual question, not around a generic profile or a broad, open-ended conversation.

The goal is simple: give you focused, experience-based insight in one efficient call so you can move forward with more confidence.

Who it's for

Built for biotech, pharma, and healthtech founders and leadership teams, especially smaller and mid-sized companies navigating high-stakes decisions without large internal teams.

  • Leaders pressure-testing valuation or commercial assumptions.
  • Founders planning launches, partnerships, or business development moves.
